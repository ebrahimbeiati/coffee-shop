Welcome to Café Sonnenschein

Welcome to Café Sonnenschein, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Basel, Smile Cafe is more than just a coffee shop; it's a haven for coffee lovers and a hub for community. With a commitment to quality, sustainability, and genuine hospitality, we invite you to join us on a journey of flavors, experiences, and unforgettable moments.

Our Story

Our story began in 1988 when two friends, Ebrahim , shared a passion for great coffee and a dream of creating a space where people could come together, relax, and connect. With determination and a love for the craft, Café Sonnenschein was born, evolving from a humble coffee cart to a beloved neighborhood institution.

Our Values

At Café Sonnenschein, our values are the foundation of everything we do. From sourcing ethically grown beans to fostering a welcoming atmosphere, we prioritize integrity, sustainability, and community. With each cup we serve, we strive to make a positive impact on our customers, our planet, and our community.

Meet Our Team

Our team at Café Sonnenschein is a family, united by our love for coffee and dedication to providing exceptional service. From our baristas crafting the perfect brew to our chefs preparing delicious pastries, each member plays a vital role in creating the Smile Cafe experience. Get to know the faces behind the counter!
